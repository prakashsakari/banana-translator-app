Fun fact

Minion speak or Banana language is spoken by the minions in the movie Despicable Me. Minionese, as it's known, is a language created by Despicable Me co-directors Pierre Coffin and Chris Renaud. It's called Banana Language because of the Minions love of the yellow-skinned fruit, this language is a combination of several real languages, including French, Italian, Spanish, Indian, Japanese, and English.
